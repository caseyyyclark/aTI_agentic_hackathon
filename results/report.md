This is common practice for large supercells but should be noted. For higher accuracy, ENCUT=520+ eV is recommended for Ge.

### 4.2 NELM=300 in 90% Sn Calculation
The 90% Sn run had `NELM=300` while the others used `NELM=100`. The run converged in 40 iterations, but the elevated limit suggests caution.

### 4.3 No Ionic Relaxation (NSW=0)
All three are **single-point calculations** — the atomic positions were not relaxed. For amorphous supercells generated by random atom placement, an energy minimization step (NSW>0, IBRION=2 or MD quench) would typically be performed before extracting properties. The reported energies and band gaps are for the **as-built** (unrelaxed) structures.
